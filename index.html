About SYNTHESIS

SYNTHESIS 2025 is our college's premier annual cultural and technical fest. It's a platform where creativity meets innovation, bringing together students from various disciplines to showcase their skills, compete, and celebrate. Get ready for a weekend filled with exhilarating competitions, captivating performances, and unforgettable moments. Join us as we synthesize ideas and create a legacy.
